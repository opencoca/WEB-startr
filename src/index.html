{# ── Storytelling intro ── #}

Storytelling is how we learn

A well-told story can teach a child to code, help a community break the cycle of poverty, or turn a side project into something people care about. At Startr we build open-source tools and platforms that make it easier for anyone to start telling that story.

{# ── Education & community ── #}

Stories that connect communities

We work alongside educators, artists, nonprofits, and makers. Sage.Education meets learners where they are with AI-powered tools. The Freedom from Poverty Foundation helps people write new chapters through education and opportunity. QMEA-AEMQ amplifies the story that music education matters across Quebec.

{# ── Creative makers ── #}

Stories told through making

Robot in a Can puts kits and coding activities in the hands of creative kids, helping them discover how things work. Classic Lofi gives independent artists a self-hosted platform to share music that tells stories without words. Wood Ring Studio crafts rings where every piece of wood carries its own story.

{# ── Tools ── #}

The tools behind the stories

Startr.Style is a CSS framework under 80 KB that keeps markup readable and design intentional. Startr.Sh simplifies standing up new webapps so teams can focus on content. Startr.Space provides flexible virtual meeting rooms for remote collaboration. And Startr.Team keeps it all running — humans and automation working together.
